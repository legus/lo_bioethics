Famous Sentences

Let´s look at some famous sentences by some famous thinkers and let´s get close to the deep feeling of what they were trying to convey.

We live in the world when we love. Living for the others is perhaps the only way life is worth living.

A country or a civilization can be judged by the way it treats animals.

We can judge the heart of men by the way they treat animals.

Mankind has an urgent need for a new wisdom in order for man to survive and improve the quality of life.
